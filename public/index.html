Hello, World!

The evolution of web design started from very humble monochrome roots. Everything really started with the dawn of browsers capable of displaying images. These first websites were still very simple but exciting. Javascript saved us from the world of tables, and made design even more exciting. Next came Flash, and it was good, but resource heavy. Apple decided Flash needed to go, so they refused to make the iphone and ipad capable of viewing Flash. Adobe was sad, but Flash has been dying a slow death ever since.

Now web design seems to have come full circle. Websites have been streamlined, and design has moved back to a simpler, cleaner time with the added benefit of being able to be viewed in any number of browsers on any device.
